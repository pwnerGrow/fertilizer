4. Care Guidelines for Each Growth Stage (with Defoliation)

Stage Care Instructions
Veg Days (1–10)Focus on strong root/leaf growth; defoliate older or damaged leaves.
Early Bloom (1–10)Gradually increase light intensity; defoliate leaves that block flower sites.
Bulking P1 (11–20)Support flower growth; perform light defoliation to improve airflow.
Bulking P2 (21–42)Reduce nitrogen; heavy defoliation to enhance light penetration.
Late Bloom P1 (43–49)Focus on flower quality; minimal defoliation to avoid stress.
Late Bloom P2 (50–53)Prepare for harvest; remove dying leaves.
Flush (54–56)Flush system; no defoliation required.
